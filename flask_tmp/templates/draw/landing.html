Welcome to Annot8

Features:

  • Annotate your images with segmentation masks
  • Save your annotations to a secure backend and pick up where you left off!
  • No Installation required!

Instructions:

  • Click the "Draw" button to enter drawing mode. New vertices can be placed by clicking on the image. A polygon is completed by clicking on the original vertex.
  • In "Edit" mode, existing vertices can be repositioned by clicking and dragging.
  • The "Save" button will save the existing polygons to a database.
  • The "Load" button will update the image with the last set of saved annotations.
